Appointment Enquiry and Booking

Welcome to GMFLORES Hospital for Online Appointment Booking. Retrieval of Appointments Within the Next 60 Days You can now receive your appointment details for the next 60 days via SMS. To retrieve your appointment details, send the following: GMF[Space]NRIC/HRN/FIN Number[Space]DOB in DD/MM/YYYY to 77227. Example: CGHAPPT S1234567A 20/01/1950

Please note that:

Standard SMS charges by your mobile service provider apply for messages sent and received from your mobile phone. Only SMS requests from the registered handphone number tagged to the respective patient will be processed. Make a New Appointment To make a new appointment to see our doctor, please click on "Make a new appointment". You'll need to fill in your details and preferred date for the appointment. Our staff will contact you by the next working day to confirm it.

Reschedule or Cancel an Appointment

To make changes to an existing appointment, please click on "Reschedule or cancel an appointment". Please note that some appointments are NOT available in the Online Appointment Booking System: Diagnostic Radiology appointment (X-rays, CT Scans, MRIs) Clinical Measurement Unit appointments Any subsequent consultations with our SOC doctors, who will require the scan / test results from the above-mentioned appointments. Please take your medication as prescribed and adhere to the advice of our medical staff. Should you run out of medication before your rescheduled specialist outpatient appointment or if you require any assistance, please contact our Appointment Centre at 6850 3333.

Reschedule or cancel an appointment

You are required to have a Singpass ID to reschedule or cancel your appointment. Click here to register if you do not have a SingPass. You may also wish to book an appointment through our Appointment Centre at 6850 3333.
